Books are media that help humans gain knowledge and understanding. Even today there is technology involved and more online media can be accessed. But nowadays, reading from a book is still popular. According to Statista Advertising & Media Outlook, global book market revenue in 2024 is forecast to be US$8,405 million. The average growth rate of book income between 2024 and 2029 is 0.56% per year. There is additional information from the publisher 'Por Phatthana' who gave an interview in Thairath media, revealing that paper is more expensive because most of it is imported. Entering from abroad The price of paper has been continually adjusted. As a result, the cost per book is approximately 4 - 5% higher than before. As a result, the price may need to be increased by 5 - 10% according to market forces and from the 2022 book fair statistics of the National Association of Publishers and Booksellers. Thailand (PUBAT) reports that most Thai readers have a purchasing power of books in the range of 500 - 1,000 baht/month. Most of the customer base is in the age range of 23 - 28 years and has an income in the range of 15,000 - 25,000 baht. The growth of the minimum wage over the past ten years The rate increased by approximately 13 - 36 baht/day. In conclusion, the price of books increased in a way that was not balanced by the slightly higher salary. The book looks expensive. And it makes many people think carefully before buying each book. The above data shows that the trend of reading books in physical form is becoming more popular. Meanwhile, the price of books has also increased. As a result, many people are hesitant to buy new books.

The Thantawan Rent Book application is an application that allows readers to access books via the Internet without having to travel to a book rental store. It will be an online platform with a membership system or subscription service where users can access various books, whether old or new. In the online format, users can choose to use this application by paying a rental fee or subscription for the desired period. And each member can use the book rental service with no limit on the number of books or types of books that can be accessed for a specified time.
